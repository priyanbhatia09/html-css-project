SIR VIV RICHARDS

Viv Richards, in full Sir Isaac Vivian Alexander Richards, (born March 7, 1952, St. John’s, Antigua), West Indian cricketer,
arguably the finest batsman of his generation.In the process of scoring 8,540 runs and
averaging more than 50 runs per innings in Test competition, Richards recorded
24 centuries (100 runs in a single innings), leading the West Indian team that he captained
from 1985 to 1991 to sweeping international success. Richards was also an “off-spin”
bowler and took 32 wickets in Test competition.
But it was as a batsman that the “Master Blaster".
